    ABOUT US

    The Humanity Tech (THT) is a Not for Profit Platform that Provides Technology Consulting, Expertise and Solutions to other Not for profit organizations at minimal or no costs with the objective to help partner organization scale their initiatives for greater social impact. 

     

    Over last few decades technology has permeated in every aspect of the society. Be it healthcare or education or finding jobs or natural calamities or simple daily life needs, technology has played its role in providing access to millions of people at the costs that were once unthinkable. While NGOs  understand the physical ground reality and have the resources for last mile delivery, they often lack the skills and funds to tap innovative technology solutions that can further amplify their initiatives. 

     

    THT aims to bridge this gap by becoming NGOs technology partner. For selected projects and initiatives, THT experts will engage with the NGO to provide technology consulting and guidance, or take it a step further and create a fully functional technology solutions at no or affordable costs. THT being an NGO itself, you can be rest assured that our objectives are aligned with yours and only reward we seek is a measurable impact by being your technology partner in your journey.
